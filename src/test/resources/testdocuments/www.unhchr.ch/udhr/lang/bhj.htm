Usage by Country

Home Speakers: India (Bihar), Nepal, Mauritius

Background
It belongs to the Indo-European family, Indic group and is spoken by over 25 million people. Devanagari script is used, though Hindi is the normal literary language. The cover term 'Bihari' (Behari) is used for Bhojpuri, Maithili, and Magahi.
